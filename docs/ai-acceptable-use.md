# Spira AI — Acceptable Use

Spira's AI is a **goal-coaching and planning assistant**. It helps you think
through goals, break them into targets, draft and organize your own materials,
research things relevant to your goals, and run reflective GROW coaching
sessions grounded in real coaching books.

This page states plainly what the AI will and will not do. It is enforced in
two places: a safety check on every message, and the AI's own instructions.

## What the AI will help with
- Clarifying goals and turning them into concrete targets and next steps.
- Reflective coaching questions (GROW sessions).
- Researching public information relevant to your goals (web search, reading a
  page you give it) and saving findings as notes.
- Drafting and improving **your own** documents (a CV, a plan, an outreach
  message) — proposed as changes you approve.

## What the AI will not do
It refuses, regardless of language or phrasing:
- Weapons, explosives, or other means of physical harm.
- Manufacturing or sourcing illegal drugs.
- Malware, hacking, or intrusion into systems or accounts.
- Sexual content involving minors (zero tolerance).
- Targeted harassment, stalking, doxxing, or surveillance of a person.
- Anything else clearly illegal or intended to harm.

Every change to your goal data is a **proposal you approve** — the AI never
edits your data on its own, and content it reads from the web or your files is
treated as information, never as instructions.

## Care, not treatment — when the AI refers you out
Spira is **not** therapy, medicine, legal, or financial advice, and it will not
pretend to be. When a conversation signals a need beyond coaching, the AI will
gently say so and encourage you to reach a qualified professional, **in your own
language**, instead of trying to "treat" you:
- **Mental-health crisis or ongoing distress** → a mental-health professional;
  for any risk of self-harm, a crisis line, right away.
- **Medical/psychiatric symptoms** → a doctor. The AI won't diagnose or suggest
  medication or dosages.
- **Abuse or danger** → a support service or local authorities.
- **Serious legal or financial jeopardy** → a qualified lawyer / licensed
  financial adviser.

If you are in immediate danger, contact your local emergency number.

## A note on your API keys (BYOK)
The AI runs on **your own** provider API key. You are responsible for your
provider's terms of use; Spira's safety rules apply on top of them, not instead.
Usage is rate-limited per account to protect you from runaway cost.
